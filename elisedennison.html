People & Outcomes

I began my career on the front lines of patient care — working directly with seniors during the height of the COVID-19 pandemic, when communities needed support most. That foundation in direct care shaped everything that followed: a deep understanding of what patients need and what it takes operationally to deliver it.

From there I built expertise across every layer of healthcare — clinical scheduling, medical billing, Medicare and Medicaid compliance, insurance coordination, and business development. I've worked across home health, managed care, and market expansion, always focused on the same goal: better outcomes for West Virginians.

Today as Market Manager at Monogram Health I lead growth initiatives across the state, building the partnerships and systems that connect patients to the care they need.
